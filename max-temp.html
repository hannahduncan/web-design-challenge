Max Temperature



Visualizations



This plot of Max Temperature vs. Latitude confirms the commonly-held hypothesis that temperature increases as we approach the equator.

Namely, as one increases in latitude from -100 to 0° and decreases from 100 to 0°, maximum temperature for August 28, 2020 increased proportionally.
