Humidity



From this graph of Humidity vs. Latitude, there appears to be no significant correlation between the two parameters.

Interestingly, for nearly all latitudes present in the dataset, there existed at least one city with humidity within the 80-100% range. This could be attributed to the following observation: as shown in the Additional Images, the dataset biased cities in the Northern Hemisphere. In this region, the month of August takes place during the height of summer, which is often known for hotter, more humid climates.
